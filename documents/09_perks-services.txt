Public Service Loan Forgiveness Program
U.S. Department of Education
MOHELA
633 Spirit Drive
Chesterfield, MO 63005-1243
Telf: 1-855-265-4038
Fax: 866-222-7060
The
Public Service Loan Forgiveness
(PSLF) Program forgives the remaining balance on your Direct Loans after 120 qualifying monthly payments have been made under a qualifying repayment plan while working full-time for a qualifying employer. You must meet your employer’s definition of full-time. If you are employed by a government or not-for-profit organization, you may be able to receive loan forgiveness under the PSLF Program.
How do I know I’m on the right track to receive PSLF?
Because you have to make 120 qualifying monthly payments, it will take at least 10 years for you to become eligible for PSLF. To help you determine if you are on the right track as early as possible, we have created an
Employment Certification for Public Service Loan Forgiveness form
that you can submit periodically while you are working toward meeting the PSLF eligibility requirements. We will use the information you provide on the form to let you know if you are making qualifying PSLF payments.

Dementia Care and Cure Initiative (DCCI) Miami Task Force
offers support and resources to members of the community caring for a loved one with symptoms or a diagnosis of Alzheimer’s or dementia. The DCCI Miami Task Force helps families navigate the challenges of living with a memory disorder. To learn more about the Miami DCCI Taskforce check out their
program flyer
and visit their
website
for additional information.
Southwest Focal Point Community Center
601 City Center Way Pembroke Pines, FL 33025
Ph: (954) 450-1060
The Carl Shechter Southwest Focal Point Community Center is the central location in Pembroke Pines. Their
Adult Day Care
is a community-based service that assists frail and dementia-specific adults to remain living in their own homes with their family, or in an assisted living care situation for as long as possible. Services are also provided for seniors who do not wish to be alone at home and who want to participate and enjoy stimulating recreational activities.
To learn more about this facility and services, visit:
https://www.ppines.com/211/Southwest-Focal-Point-Community-Center
