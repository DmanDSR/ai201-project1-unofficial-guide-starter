Student Food Pantry
The food pantries serve FIU students in need. There are two pantry locations
and they are donations based. All the food distributed comes from donations made by staff, faculty, members of the community, and student groups. Students do not need to provide proof of need to use the facility.
The student food pantry provides non-perishable food items including canned proteins, fruits, dairy, vegetables, and soups, along with grains, oils and condiments. Toiletry items are also provided in our pantry.
Support the Student Food Pantry
Visit the food pantry
The pantry is available to all currently enrolled FIU students. Proof of need is not required and students can visit once a week, taking 8 pounds of food per visit.
Be sure to bring your FIU Onecard for access.
